## The Premise

Every thirty years, the lord of Ashvale feeds a human soul to the forest. In return, the crops grow. The wells run sweet. The plagues stay away.

This year, the lord refused.

Now the crops are dying. The water tastes of poison. A priest preaches divine punishment while a thieves' guild plots in the shadows. An army gathers on the horizon. And deep in the Thornwood, something ancient is losing patience.

**You arrive carrying a dead man's compass. It hasn't pointed north in thirty years.**

## в”Җв”Җв”Җ The Weight of Choice в”Җв”Җв”Җ

</div>

> *There are no good choices in Ashvale. Only less terrible ones.*

A single decision вҖ” burning the grain silo вҖ” cascades through **six systems** over **45 in-game days**:

```
Day  0  в”Ӯ Silo burns. Guards hostile. Bounty placed.
Day  3  в”Ӯ Food supply drops 30%. Bread price +50%.
Day 14  в”Ӯ FAMINE declared. Prices triple. Villagers starve.
Day 21  в”Ӯ NPCs blame outsiders. Elara remembers. Village fractures.
Day 30  в”Ӯ Peasant revolt. Lord's power collapses. Guard desertions.
Day 45  в”Ӯ IF lord's power < 20% вҶ’ Ironmarch Legion invades.
```

The player who **helped Elara investigate** instead sees trust building, evidence gathering, a midnight scream, a family reunion that breaks three people, and a forest that starts to listen.

**No two playthroughs are the same.**
